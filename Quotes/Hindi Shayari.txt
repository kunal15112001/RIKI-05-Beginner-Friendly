SHAYARI 1:
Adhi raat jo ya barsat ho rahi hai
Lagta hai firr kuch adhuri baat ho rahi hai...
Suna rahe hai ambar zameen ko hale dil
Is tarha yun dono kii mulakat ho rahi hai...
Kuch is tarha baras rahe hai ye badal
Bundo mai uski vo yaad kho rahi hai...
Bin mausam na jane q aaj firr ya barsat ho rahi hai....



SHAYARI 2:
Aj k bad phir nahi hogi 
Ye hansi raat phir nahi hogi 
Aise badal to phir bhi ayenge par ye barsat phir nahi hogi
Or ak nazar mud k dekhne wale kee ye khairat phir nahi hogi...



SHAYARI 3:
Waqt gujar jata h kahani bankar.
Yaad rah jati h sirf nishani bankar.
Hum to humesh dil ke karib rahenge.
Kabhi mosam to kabhi aakho ka pani bankar.


SHAYARI 4:
Dukh de ke sawaal karte ho,
tum bhi galib kamaal karte ho... 
Dekh ker puchh liya haal mera chalo kuch to khayaal karte ho....
Sher - dil mein udasiya kesi yeh bhi mujhse sawaal karte ho,
Marna chahe to mar nahi sakte tum bhi jeena muhaal karte ho...
Kis kis ki misaal du tumko her sitam bemisaal karte ho.


SHAYARI 5:
Iss Qadr Naa Har Baat, 
Yaaron Se Puucho,
Jo Baat Raaz Ki Ho,
 iSharoon Se Puucho ..
Lehroon Se Khelna To, 
Samandar Ka Shauk Hai,
Lagti Hai Chot Kaise, 
Ye Kinaroon Se Puucho…
